TradeGrip ✊

TradeGrip is beneficial for active day trading, facilitating better context to trades and quick access to data. The ticker search feature saves valuable time, and the checklist may bring to mind aspects of trading that were not thought of despite having an understanding of the given concept.

• Finviz is popular for various fundamental & share count data
• FloatChecker is convenient for float & SI info
• MarketWatch is handy for pre/post-session vol
• Stocktwits is valuable for rumors & moves in lieu of news
• TradingView is useful for TA stats & a convenient chart
• Twitter is essential for sentiment & some origins of news

The following is a familiarity checklist to bring to mind contextual aspects of trading that you may already understand, but might not have thought of when entering this given trade:

Volatility
Relative potential for the price to plummet on 1m chart

Some stocks may go down the entire day but never have a big move at any given
minute. Knowing the likelihood of this is important and there are often behavioral
hints that are even worth waiting for.

Are you satisfied with your familiarity with this stock regarding...? (+4)

Exit level
At what level you can identify that you are wrong

You can't know anything 100%. The best traders know it is about probability,
not predictability. Do you know ahead of time where to get out?

Are you satisfied with your familiarity with this stock regarding...? (+3)

Time
Time of day significance of potential price action

Whether it's in the premarket, near the market open, later in the day or after the
4pm close, the exact same stock with same news can behave differently. Some
stocks typically experience no volume before 9:30am and after 4:00pm.

Are you satisfied with your familiarity with this stock regarding...? (+1)

Warrants
Are there warrants for this stock?

There's usually another ticker with a 'W' at the end or '/WS' sometimes such as on
TDA. If warrants exist it's a good idea to check at what price they are exercisable.
To find that you can check their most recent financial report.

Are you satisfied with your familiarity with this stock regarding...? (+2)

Short interest
% of publicly tradeable shares sold short

How much short interest the stock has, or any tendency of it to increase
or decrease around time intervals can be taken to mean negative things,
and if unexpectedly wrong can be positive for the stock.

Are you satisfied with your familiarity with this stock regarding...? (+2)

Multiyear chart & vol
The long-term chart & respective volume

Always zoom out to get the bigger picture. There may be no direct correlation
between it going from $100 -> $1 over the last two years and what it will do today,
but it is important to get as many puzzle pieces in place as you can.
